This is XiaoGo

An

AI robotic trash classifier which can detect sea coast waste
designed by the SeeSea - See Change project Open FIESTA team.

The problem


Ocean debris – Almost 300,000 tons of ocean waste, float on the surface, degrading and sinking the world’s coasts, adding to the great environmental concern of plastic waste in the oceans.


While volunteering work has greatly contributed overtime to collect and sort out sea waste - in its majority mostly plastic debris -

XiaoGo car can help to further detect, pick up and sort out trash, in a larger scale, that can be hazardous for people, the environment and the biodiversity.

XiaoGo

can detect 4 sea coast trash categories; dry garbage, wet garbage (kitchen waste), recyclables and hazardous waste.
XiaoGo can sort them into the corresponding bins, according to Shanghai’s first regulation on domestic waste management in 2018.
